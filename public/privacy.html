If you enable Google AdSense, Google may use cookies and/or device identifiers to serve and measure ads. You may be required to show a consent banner depending on your users' location.

If you enable purchases, payments are processed by a payment provider (e.g. Lemon Squeezy). We do not store your full card details on our servers.
