Privacy Policy

Product: Stellar Siege (web game)

This policy explains what data is processed when you use Stellar Siege, why it is processed, and what controls are available to users. By using the game, you agree to this policy.

1. Data We Process

  • Local gameplay data stored in your browser (for example: settings, progress, upgrades, and local scores).
  • Account data if you choose sign-in (for example: user identifier, cloud-synced progression, and leaderboard records).
  • Operational logs required for security, anti-abuse, and service reliability.

2. Why We Use Data

  • To run core game functionality and preserve progression between sessions.
  • To support optional account sync and online duel features.
  • To detect abuse, maintain stability, and improve gameplay quality.

3. Ads And Measurement

Ads may be served by Google services. These services can use cookies or similar identifiers to measure ad performance, reduce invalid traffic, and personalize ad delivery where permitted by law. Ads are not guaranteed on every page.

Depending on user location, consent or additional notices may be required before personalized ads are shown.

4. Payments

If purchases are enabled, payment transactions are handled by external payment processors. We do not store full payment card numbers on Stellar Siege servers.

5. Data Retention

Browser-local data remains on your device until you clear site storage. Account-linked cloud data is retained while the account is active, or longer when required for legal, fraud prevention, or security purposes.

6. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are applied to protect service data. No internet service can provide absolute security; users should keep their devices and browsers updated.

7. User Controls

  • You can clear browser storage to remove local saved data.
  • You can sign out to stop account-linked cloud sync.
  • You can adjust browser cookie and site-permission settings.

8. Children

Stellar Siege is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law in your region without parental or guardian involvement.

9. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect product, legal, or infrastructure changes. Material updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date on this page.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: support@stellarsiege.com.
