What Is Stellar Siege?

Stellar Siege is a browser-based roguelite arena shooter. Each run starts simple and grows into higher pressure combat through enemy scaling, objective changes, and build choices. The game is designed for quick sessions, while still rewarding players who want to optimize ships and strategy over time.

Core Modes

  • Survival - Endless wave pressure, best for score pushing and credits farming.
  • Campaign - Objective-driven missions that unlock progression and improve mechanical skill.
  • Online Duel - Competitive mode where two players race to break the opponent hull first.

Controls

  • Move with WASD or arrow keys.
  • Aim with mouse.
  • Shoot with left click or Space.
  • Use fullscreen for improved visibility on high-wave encounters.

Progression Systems

  • Credits - Main currency earned through active gameplay and mission completion.
  • Crystals - Premium progression currency used for selected ships and advanced upgrades.
  • XP - Pilot progression that improves long-term account strength.
  • Ship Upgrades - Permanent improvements that change weapon feel and survivability.

Online Duel Rules

  • Both players join the same room code.
  • Each ship displays clear labels so players can track who is who in combat.
  • Win condition: reduce opponent hull to zero before your own is destroyed.

Practical Strategy Tips

  • Do not chase every target. Keep movement smooth and maintain safe firing lanes.
  • Use short strafes instead of long drifts to avoid overcommitting into enemy volleys.
  • In harder waves, prioritize threat control over score greed.
  • Build consistency first, then speed. Stable runs produce stronger long-term progression.
